Welcome to the Monty Hall Game under a memory load.

This experiment was prepared to study people's decision making strategies under a memory load.

You will play Monty Hall Game after a memory task.

The memory task is a 4x4 matrix with randomly positions dots on it. You will see the matrix only for 1 second.You will have only 1 second to memorise them.


Then you will play the Monty Hall Game. After your decision is recorded, an empty matrix will be presented. You will be asked to place the dots on the matrix as much as you can remember.


There is no time limit for the experiment
