Welcome to the memory and decision task

This experiment was prepared to study people's decision making strategies under a memory condition

The memory condition contains a matrix with 4 randomly positioned dots.

Before the decision making task, you will see the matrix with randomly placed dots on it for 850ms

Then you will read a scenario and make a decision about it. After your decision is recorded an empty matrix will be presented. You will be asked to place the dots on the matrix correctly.

There is no time limit for the experiment

Can you remember these dots?
